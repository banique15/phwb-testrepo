# Process Payroll Workflow

## Overview
Guide for processing artist payments and compensation.

## Prerequisites
- Artist assignments completed
- Hours and rates recorded
- Payment period defined
- Approval permissions available

## Steps
1. Navigate to Payroll page
2. Select payment period
3. Review artist assignments
4. Verify hours and rates
5. Calculate compensation
6. Generate payment records
7. Process payments
8. Record completion

## Payroll Components
- Artist hourly rates
- Event assignments
- Hours worked
- Payment calculations
- Tax considerations

## Success Criteria
- Payments calculated accurately
- Records properly maintained
- Artists compensated correctly
