# Track Compensation Workflow

## Overview
Guide for tracking and managing artist compensation records.

## Prerequisites
- Artist assignments exist
- Payment rates established
- Hours tracking system in place

## Steps
1. Access Payroll tracking
2. Review artist assignments
3. Update hours worked
4. Verify compensation rates
5. Track payment status
6. Generate compensation reports

## Compensation Tracking
- Assignment details
- Hours worked
- Hourly rates
- Total compensation
- Payment status
- Historical records

## Success Criteria
- Compensation accurately tracked
- Payment records maintained
- Reports available for review
