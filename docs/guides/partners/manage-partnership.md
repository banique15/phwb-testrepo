# Manage Partnership Workflow

## Overview
Guide for managing ongoing partnerships and agreements.

## Prerequisites
- Partner relationship established
- Partnership terms defined

## Steps
1. Access partner profile
2. Review partnership status
3. Update agreement terms
4. Track partnership activities
5. Manage communications
6. Document partnership outcomes

## Partnership Management
- Agreement tracking
- Communication history
- Activity logging
- Performance metrics
- Renewal planning

## Success Criteria
- Partnership properly maintained
- Communications documented
- Performance tracked
